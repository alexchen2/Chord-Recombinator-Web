Select three or more notes from the panels above, then press the identify button to find the chords. To delete a note, hover over or tap on the panel to bring up a clickable popup.

OR

After clicking the "Add Note" button on the left side, click/tap & drag on the staff to add notes. To remove notes from the staff, click on the trashbin button and click/tap & drag on the note in question.

When neither the add or remove notes button are pressed down, click on a note to select it, then use the pitch and accidental modification buttons on the right to alter the note.
